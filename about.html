Our Mission

ECAI is a well-structured and dynamic association that brings together education consultancy professionals from every state across India. It serves as a robust B2B connection platform designed to foster collaboration, networking, and professional growth among its members. ECAI stands as a cornerstone for education consultants in India, empowering them through a structured, supportive, and ethically-driven network that promotes excellence in the field of education counselling.

What Makes ECAI Stand Out?

  • Nationwide Representation
    • With members from all states, ECAI offers a truly pan-Indian network, ensuring diverse insights and regional expertise within the education consultancy landscape.
  • B2B Connection Platform
    • ECAI facilitates strong business-to-business relationships, enabling consultants to share best practices, forge partnerships, and collaborate on initiatives that enhance the overall quality of education counseling services.
  • Member Welfare and Support
    • The association is dedicated to the welfare of its members. It provides comprehensive support that includes professional development programs, industry updates, ethical guidelines, and a range of welfare initiatives designed to help consultants thrive.
  • Inclusivity of Domestic and Abroad Consultants
    • ECAI welcomes both domestic and international education consultants. This inclusive approach ensures a global perspective, allowing members to benefit from cross-border insights, resources, and collaborative opportunities.
