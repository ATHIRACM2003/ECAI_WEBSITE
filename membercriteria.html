Membership Criteria

Eligibility Requirements

  1. Professional Experience
    • - Applicants must be actively engaged in education counseling.
    • - A minimum of three (3) years of experience in the field is preferable..
    • - They should have demonstrable expertise in guiding students on academic and career pathways.
  2. Ethical Standards & Track Record
      - Applicants must uphold the highest ethical standards in their counseling practices.
      - A proven track record of ethical, transparent, and student-centric counseling is essential.
      - Any history of misleading guidance, fraudulent activities, or unethical behavior may result in disqualification.
  3. Membership Fees
      - Membership Fee (India-based members): ₹10,000 (one-time payment).
      - Annual Renewal Fee:₹2,000.
      - Fees ensure access to exclusive resources, professional networking, and continuous professional development opportunities.
  4. Admission & Verification Process
      - Each membership application will undergo a verification process conducted by the organization.
      - The applicant’s credentials, professional experience, and ethical track record will bereviewed and verified
      - The final decision to approve membership rests with the organization’s governing body.
      - The organization reserves the right to accept or decline applications based on its evaluation.

By fulfilling these criteria, members gain recognition as trusted education consultants , contributing to a professional and ethical counselling community.
