Know about the Event Partner of ECAI

Edu Carnival Expo:

Edu Carnival is the event partner for ECAI, playing a crucial role in organizing and managing the educational expo. As the dedicated event partner, Edu Carnival brings its expertise in delivering high-quality educational events that connect institutions, professionals, and students. This collaboration ensures a seamless and impactful experience for all participants, offering valuable networking opportunities, showcasing the latest innovations in education, and promoting knowledge sharing within the industry. Edu Carnival's partnership with ECAI highlights its strong presence and commitment to shaping the future of education, contributing significantly to the growth and development of the education sector.
