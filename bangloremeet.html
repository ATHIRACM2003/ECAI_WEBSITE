ECAI Bangalore Meet 2025

Connecting education consultants for a brighter future.

About ECAI Bangalore Meet 2025

The ECAI Bangalore Meet, set for March 2025, is a one-day event that connects over 300 verified education consultants from across India. The event will host more than 500 candidates and parents visiting institutional stalls. It’s designed to facilitate high-level networking, strategic B2B meetings, and the exploration of academic opportunities.

Attendees will engage in discussions on the latest trends in education and collaborate to explore new avenues for academic growth. The event’s strategic B2B meetings will foster valuable partnerships between consultants and educational institutions. The Edu Carnival 2025 Expo will feature institutional stalls showcasing courses, scholarships, and campus facilities, offering direct engagement opportunities for students and parents.

Key Components

All-India Conference:

  • Consultants-Only: 300 top-tier, verified consultants will gather to discuss trends and share best practices.

B2B Meetings:

  • Consultant-to-Consultant: Network and exchange insights.
  • Consultant-to-Institution: Build partnerships for enhanced admission strategies.

Edu Carnival Education Expo:

  • Institutional Stalls: Domestic and international institutions will showcase courses, scholarships, and campus facilities.
  • Candidate & Parent Engagement: Direct interactions to explore academic and career opportunities.

Why Attend?

Networking:

  • Connect with industry experts and institutional leaders.

Growth:

  • Leverage B2B sessions for strategic collaborations.

Insights:

  • Gain direct access to the latest trends, academic offerings, and admission opportunities.

Join us at Edu Carnival 2025 to help shape the future of education in India!
